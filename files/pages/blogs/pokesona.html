my history with pokemon (and the reason for this blog)

soooo, as a kid i was pretty into pokemon. (at least, as much as someone can be having never played a pokemon game...) i watched the anime and i had a pokedex. i also had pokemon rumble on my wii, but it was only the demo so i just went through that portion of the game over and over. though once my mom rented pokepark wii: pikachu's adventure from gamefly and it was the best week of my life! good times...

anywho, i'm not big into pokemon now but i've had good times with it. recently, i've been interested in making a pokesona. no particular reason, i just think it could be cool. but there are like, *checks notes* 1025 pokemon????? last i checked there were 725 o-0 point is, way too many to choose from. consequently, i'll be listing some here and their qualities to make them a good pokesona. if i ever end up deciding and making a pokesona, i'll put it here.
