Computer Mii Maker

How to make Miis on your computer and things you can do with it.

Making A Mii on your Computer

  1. Log in on Nintendo's website.
  2. Go to Mii Studio
  3. Make a Mii and save it!

Saving A Mii Headshot

  1. Go to Mii Studio
  2. Click outside of your browser to unfocus the application window
  3. Right-click on the Mii
  4. Save to computer

Making a Spinning GIF of your Mii

  2. Go to the editor of the Mii you want to spin around
  3. Click Finish
  4. Right-click your Mii and open inspect element
  5. Open div tags until you get to the image link of your Mii that looks like a sprite sheet
