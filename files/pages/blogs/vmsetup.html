Printer Trouble

I've been at work which means I get access to many old things! There are some old laptops in a storage room that i'm itching to get my hands on, but those are for another time...

In true Angel fashion, I've tinkered around the office and messed with some old stuff. Right now, the object of my attention is a Canon imageRUNNER 2525. It was just sitting unused on one of the floors when my boss mentioned it was broken. He never specified exactly how it was broken, just that Canon wouldn't dispose of it or send someone to fix it. So I decided to tinker around and see what specifically was broken. After a bit of trial and error, I managed to get it on and working! I tested the copy feature and it wrinkled up the paper it spit out, but the print job itself was decent. The whole thing is full of ink, and it even has a stapler feature!
Today we tried printing a board meeting packet and began to run into issues... it seems the rollers are super borked and wrinkle every paper they come across. You'll only get through a few pages of a print job before running into a paper jam (which is only actually there like half of the time??). So I'll have to figure out if I can fix that later. Scanning works fine though! It's just reallyyyyyyy slow. Scans to USB with minimal issues other than the occasional paperjam with weirdly shaped papers. A win is a win!
