my music

during middle school i made music in garageband. using liveloops to make an album (which i still have! archive your stuff!!) was my introduction to "making music". i don't make music that much anymore, because i have a hard time creating something i like.

every once-in-a-while, i get an idea for a song. if i make something decent, i'll upload it to soundcloud and link it here. been trying to make a bossa nova song for ages, i have a cute concept to make an ep out of bunny robot song.

a lullaby for a princess (bunny robot song) - cyberangel
i made this song for a class at school. it is very robotic and simple because i was focused on the melody.

story: in a floating castle not too far from here resides a bunny robot. she is old and nearly nonfunctional, but sometimes you can hear her gentle melody floating down to greet you. it is said that long ago she was the caretaker of the children residing in that castle, before it rose from the ground. in the heights of their reign, the royal family had supported all kinds of creative endeavours favored by their subjects. they were the most colorful and technologically advanced kingdom for miles. unfortunately, this mighty kingdom fell. no one knows why, but all of the residents had dissappeared and the castle began to float into the sky. all of the robots and art were left behind. one-by-one, the art decayed and the robots deactivated. the only thing left is the bunny robot, singing her calm melody until her last moments.
