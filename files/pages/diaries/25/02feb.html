2-9-25

listening: can opener's notebook: fish whisperer - vylet pony
feeling: chill

hi! yesterday i traveled a bit to go to kamicon with my bf and had a ton of fun. we mostly stayed in the arcade and the artists alley the whole time, playing games and browsing. i ended up purchasing 5 things, a new record! i got a princess luna keychain, a rei ayanami keychain, two rei ayanami prints, and a rei shirt. i wonder who my favorite character is.

my favorite part of kami con continues to be the arcade. everything inside is free to play, and there's a huge variety of games. you can play fighting games, rhythm games, dancing games, pinball, vr, retro consoles, and one game that was like simulating being a train conductor?? that one looked cool but i didn't have time to play it. i played tekken, guilty gear, bubble bobble, a handful of rhythm games (including taiko no tatsujin, with the drums and everything), and jet grind radio. we watched a tekken tournament before we left and it was actually pretty exciting by the end.

there were a ton of cool cosplays at the con. i spent the whole time pointing out characters i recognized and my bf played where's waldo trying to find them lol. i sort of cosplayed as parappa the rapper, because i bought his hat and wore an outfit vaguely resembling his. 6 people recognized me though, so i win B) the coolest cosplay i saw was this guy dressed up as the prince from katamari damacy. he competed in a katamari tournament they were holding but idr if he won or not lol.
